The new way to interact with your data

Ask questions in natural language, and get answers in the form of interactive charts and reports.

DATAKI uses an extremely simple approach. You create widgets by asking questions. Each widget is related to a datasource, to a SQL query, and to a chart type. You can iterate on your widgets, and ask more questions to refine them.

There is no need to learn complex abstractions or domain specific languages.
